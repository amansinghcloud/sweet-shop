The Madhuraj Story

Founded in 1990 by Shri Ramesh Kumar, Madhuraj Sweet House began as a small shop dedicated to traditional, pure ghee mithai. Our secret lies in using only the finest, freshest ingredients and adhering strictly to time-honored family recipes.

For over three decades, we have proudly served generations, becoming synonymous with celebratory moments and festive joy. Our commitment to quality has never wavered.
